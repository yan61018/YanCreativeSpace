Mixed media / 2023

Digital Integration Studies

A mixed-media project that moves from photography and Photoshop composition to hand drawing and final print.

01 / Digital composition

Testing image relationships.

I used Adobe Photoshop to test layouts and combine original photos into different digital compositions. This phase explored contrast, layering, rhythm, and how separate images could create a new structure together.

Testing layout, rhythm, and image relationships in Photoshop.

02 / Drawing on top

Responding by hand.

After selecting the composition, I printed it and drew directly on top using charcoal, ink, and graphite. The marks respond to, interrupt, and transform the digital image.

Using charcoal, ink, and graphite to transform the printed image.

03 / Final print

Digital and physical, together.

The final piece brings together photography, Photoshop composition, print, and hand drawing. It reflects my interest in layering media and moving between digital and physical making.

A final mixed-media piece combining digital and physical processes.
