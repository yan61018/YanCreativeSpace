Photography · Ongoing

Visual Notes

Everyday observations across public space, family, travel, signage, light, and cultural detail.

Artist statement

I photograph what a place quietly reveals.

Photography helps me slow down and notice the visual language of everyday life: a gesture between people, a hand-painted sign, a familiar street seen from a new angle, or light changing the feeling of an ordinary scene.

Approach

Observation before explanation.

I work intuitively, using framing, color, timing, and distance to preserve a moment without over-directing it. These images also become visual research for my design and cross-cultural storytelling work.
